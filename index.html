Destinations

Africa's defining
dive frontiers.

Every destination is selected for its technical diving merit — depth, visibility, structure, and the challenge it presents.

🇲🇿

Mozambique

Quirimbas Archipelago

Pristine oceanic walls, deep pinnacles, and Raggie sharks along structures that drop beyond 80m.

🇿🇦

South Africa

Aliwal Shoal · Sodwana Bay

World-class wreck diving, Tiger shark dives, and the Cathedral — a complex technical cave system.

🇿🇼

Zimbabwe

Chinhoyi Caves

Vertical limestone shafts, deep cave passages, and unsurveyed leads in some of Africa's least-dived karst.

🇷🇼

Rwanda / DRC

Lake Kivu

High-altitude freshwater diving in a volcanically active rift lake with exceptional visibility.

🇳🇦

Namibia

Dragon's Breath · Aigamas

Cathedral-scale sinkholes, abseil-access underground lakes, and unsurveyed passages dropping past 140m.

🇸🇩

Sudan

Conshelf II · Sanganeb

Diving in Cousteau's footsteps — Conshelf II on the reef, deep wartime wrecks, and southern walls dropping below diveable depths.

🇰🇪

Kenya

Lamu · Diani

Historic wrecks, coral-encrusted drop-offs, and warm Indian Ocean water year-round.
